# MuscleMap - Executive Summary

## The Problem: Fitness Apps Have an 80% Churn Rate

Users log sets into spreadsheets and hope something's working. There's no feedback loop, no visual proof, no moment of "I can see my progress."

**Result**: People give up because progress is invisible.

---

## The Solution: MuscleMap

A fitness visualization platform that renders real-time muscle activation on a 3D anatomical model. Log a set, watch the muscle light up. Complete your weekly goal, see your entire body glow.

**It transforms abstract workout data into immediate, visceral feedback.**

---

## The Innovation: Proprietary Bias Weight System

At the core is a bias weight system that normalizes activation display across 98+ muscles:
- Your glutes need 20 sets to "fill up"
- Your rear delts need 6
- **The system knows. It shows balanced, accurate progress.**

This is something no other app does. It's your competitive moat.
