## Community Philosophy

> **Your Tribe. Your Journey. Together.**

MuscleMap isn't just about tracking workouts - it's about finding people who share your goals, celebrating wins together, and pushing each other to be better.

### Core Values

1. **Encouragement over Competition** - We lift each other up
2. **Authenticity** - No fake progress, no cheating
3. **Inclusivity** - All fitness levels welcome
4. **Respect** - Treat others as you want to be treated
5. **Growth Mindset** - Everyone is on a journey

### Finding Your Hangout

1. **Go to Locations** in the app
2. **Search** for your gym by name or address
3. **Browse** nearby gyms on the map
4. **Join** with one tap

### Creating a Hangout

If your gym doesn't exist yet:

1. Tap **"Add New Hangout"**
2. Enter the gym name
3. Add the address
4. Set the gym type:
   - Commercial Gym
   - CrossFit Box
   - Home Gym
   - Outdoor Space
   - University Gym
   - Corporate Fitness Center
5. Add a description (optional)
6. Upload a photo (optional)
7. **Become the Founder!**

### Hangout Roles

| Role | Can Do |
|------|--------|
| **Founder** | Everything + transfer ownership |
| **Admin** | Manage members, edit settings, moderate |
| **Moderator** | Remove posts, warn members |
| **Member** | Post, participate, view content |

### Hangout Features

- **Activity Feed** - See who's training and what they're doing
- **Local Leaderboards** - Compete with gym-mates
- **Check-ins** - Let others know you're at the gym
- **Events** - Organize group workouts, challenges
- **Announcements** - Gym-wide communication

## Virtual Hangouts

Not everyone has a local community. Virtual Hangouts connect people based on **interests, goals, or schedules**.

### Types of Virtual Hangouts

**By Interest:**
- Powerlifting Den
- Yoga Flow Circle
- HIIT Warriors
- Running Pack
- Bodybuilding Stage

**By Goal:**
- Weight Loss Warriors
- Muscle Builders United
- Marathon Training Squad
- Strength Foundations
- Flexibility Focus

**By Schedule:**
- 5AM Club
- Lunch Break Lifters
- Night Owls Iron
- Weekend Warriors

**By Archetype:**
- Spartans United
- Dancers Collective
- Scholar's Lab
- Warrior's Dojo

### Joining Virtual Hangouts

1. **Go to Locations > Virtual**
2. **Browse** categories or search
3. **Preview** the community activity
4. **Join** instantly

You can be in **multiple Virtual Hangouts** at once!

### Creating a Virtual Hangout

1. Tap **"Create Virtual Hangout"**
2. Choose a name and category
3. Set privacy (open/invite-only)
4. Write a description
5. Set any requirements (optional)
6. Launch your community!

### Starting a Rivalry

1. **Find an opponent:**
   - From leaderboards
   - From your Hangout
   - Search by username

2. **Set the terms:**
   - **Metric:** Total TU, Workout Count, or Specific Exercise
   - **Duration:** 3 days, 1 week, 2 weeks, or 1 month
   - **Stakes:** Credits wagered (both put up equal amounts)

3. **Send the challenge**

4. **Opponent accepts or declines**

### During a Rivalry

- Real-time score updates
- Notifications when opponent logs workout
- Trash talk (optional, keep it friendly!)
- Can't cancel once started

### Rivalry Tips

- **Match skill levels** - Challenge someone close to your stats
- **Shorter is better** - 3-7 day rivalries stay exciting
- **Don't overcommit** - Only wager what you can afford to lose
- **Stay active** - Consistency beats one big session

### Fair Play

The system has **anti-cheat measures**:
- Suspicious workout patterns flagged
- Manual review for extreme claims
- Repeat offenders banned from rivalries

### Creating a Crew

1. **Go to Crews > Create New**
2. **Name your crew** (unique, can't change later)
3. **Set crew size** (4-20 members)
4. **Choose privacy:**
   - Open - Anyone can join
   - Request - Members must be approved
   - Invite-Only - Captain invites only
5. **Write a description**
6. **Set requirements** (optional):
   - Minimum level
   - Specific archetype
   - Activity requirements

### Crew Roles

| Role | Abilities |
|------|-----------|
| **Captain** | Full control, transfer ownership, disband |
| **Officer** | Invite/remove members, manage settings |
| **Member** | Participate, view content, chat |
| **Recruit** | 7-day trial period, limited features |

### Crew Features

- **Crew Chat** - Private group messaging
- **Shared Workouts** - Train the same program
- **Crew Challenges** - Internal competitions
- **Team Goals** - Collective TU targets
- **Crew Leaderboard** - Rank among crews globally

### Crew Achievements

Earn crew-specific achievements:
- "First Crew Workout" - Train together
- "Crew Synergy" - All members active in one week
- "Crew Domination" - Top crew in local area
- "Anniversary" - Crew active for 1 year

## Messaging

Connect directly with other members.

### Direct Messages (DMs)

One-on-one private conversations:

1. **Go to someone's profile**
2. **Tap "Message"**
3. **Start chatting**

### Group Chats

Create conversations with multiple people:

1. **Go to Messages > New Group**
2. **Add members** (up to 50)
3. **Name your group**
4. **Start chatting**

### Message Features

- **Text messages** - Basic communication
- **Image sharing** - Progress pics, form checks
- **File attachments** - Workout plans, documents
- **Read receipts** - See when messages are read
- **Mute** - Silence notifications
- **Leave** - Exit any conversation

### Etiquette

- Respond in reasonable time
- Ask before sending form check videos
- Keep it fitness-related
- Report inappropriate messages

## Community Guidelines

### The Golden Rules

1. **Be Respectful**
   - No harassment, bullying, or hate speech
   - Respect different fitness levels and goals
   - Constructive feedback only

2. **Be Authentic**
   - Log real workouts only
   - Don't claim false PRs
   - Be honest about your journey

3. **Be Supportive**
   - Celebrate others' wins
   - Offer encouragement
   - Share knowledge freely

4. **Be Safe**
   - Don't share medical advice
   - Don't promote dangerous practices
   - Encourage proper form

5. **Be Appropriate**
   - No explicit content
   - No spam or self-promotion
   - Keep content fitness-focused

### Reporting Issues

See something that violates guidelines?

1. **Tap the â‹® menu** on any post/message
2. **Select "Report"**
3. **Choose reason:**
   - Harassment
   - Spam
   - Inappropriate content
   - Cheating
   - Other
4. **Add details** (optional)
5. **Submit**

Reports are reviewed by moderators within 24 hours.

### Consequences

| Violation | First Offense | Second | Third |
|-----------|---------------|--------|-------|
| Minor | Warning | 24h mute | 7-day ban |
| Moderate | 24h mute | 7-day ban | 30-day ban |
| Severe | Immediate ban | - | - |

### Appeals

Banned unfairly?
1. Go to **Settings > Support > Appeal**
2. Explain your situation
3. Wait for review (usually 48-72 hours)

## Community Tips

### Getting the Most Out of Community

1. **Be active** - Post your workouts, comment on others
2. **Join multiple groups** - Hangout + Virtual + Crew
3. **Start rivalries** - They keep you motivated
4. **Give high-fives** - Simple encouragement goes far
5. **Share your journey** - Authenticity builds connections

### Finding Your Tribe

Not sure where to fit in?

1. **Start with your archetype** - Join that Virtual Hangout
2. **Find your local gym** - Even if small, start a Hangout
3. **Try rivalries** - Competition reveals compatible partners
4. **Be patient** - Community takes time to build

### Growing Your Following

Want to inspire others?

1. **Be consistent** - Regular activity gets noticed
2. **Share insights** - Tips, lessons learned, mistakes
3. **Engage genuinely** - Comment meaningfully on others' posts
4. **Celebrate milestones** - Your wins inspire others

## Mentorship Program

### Becoming a Mentor

Experienced members can help newcomers:

**Requirements:**
- Level 20+ in your archetype
- 6+ months active
- Clean community record
- Apply and be approved

**Mentor Benefits:**
- Special badge
- Priority matching
- Exclusive mentor community
- Bonus credits for active mentoring

### Finding a Mentor

New and want guidance?

1. **Go to Community > Mentorship**
2. **Browse available mentors**
3. **Filter by:**
   - Archetype
   - Goals
   - Language
   - Schedule
4. **Request mentorship**
5. **Get matched!**

### What Mentors Do

- Answer questions
- Review workout logs
- Provide form feedback
- Offer encouragement
- Share experience
