## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Sign Up

Visit [musclemap.me/signup](https://musclemap.me/signup) or download the mobile app.

```
What you'll need:
- Email address
- Password (minimum 8 characters)
- Display name (your public username)
```

### Step 2: Verify Your Email

Check your inbox for a verification email. Click the link to activate your account.

### Step 3: Complete Onboarding

After verification, you'll go through a quick onboarding process:

1. **Select Your Goals** - What do you want to achieve?
   - Build muscle
   - Lose weight
   - Improve endurance
   - Increase flexibility
   - General fitness

2. **Choose Your Equipment** - What do you have access to?
   - Full gym
   - Home gym
   - Bodyweight only
   - Dumbbells only
   - Resistance bands

3. **Note Any Limitations** - Optional but helpful
   - Injuries
   - Mobility restrictions
   - Medical conditions

## Choosing Your Archetype

Your archetype defines your training philosophy. It doesn't limit what exercises you can do - it guides your progression and connects you with like-minded trainers.

### The 10 Archetypes

| Archetype | Focus | Best For |
|-----------|-------|----------|
| **Spartan** | Raw strength, endurance | Those who want functional power |
| **Athlete** | Balanced performance | Sports and overall fitness |
| **Dancer** | Grace, flexibility | Mobility and body control |
| **Monk** | Mind-body connection | Meditation and focused training |
| **Warrior** | Combat readiness | Martial artists, fighters |
| **Explorer** | Functional fitness | Outdoor adventurers |
| **Guardian** | Protective strength | Those protecting others |
| **Scholar** | Scientific optimization | Data-driven trainers |
| **Healer** | Recovery and longevity | Long-term health focus |
| **Artisan** | Crafted aesthetics | Physique-focused training |

### How to Choose

Ask yourself:
- What body do I admire?
- What activities do I want to excel at?
- What mindset resonates with me?

**Don't worry** - you can change your archetype later, and your progress carries over!

## Your First Workout

### Starting a Workout

1. **From the Dashboard**, tap "Start Workout"
2. **Choose workout type**:
   - **Quick Start** - Log exercises as you go
   - **Prescription** - AI-generated workout for your goals
   - **Custom Template** - Your saved workout routines

### Logging Exercises

For each exercise, enter:
- **Sets** - Number of rounds
- **Reps** - Repetitions per set
- **Weight** - Load used (optional)
- **Duration** - Time-based exercises (optional)
- **Notes** - Any comments (optional)

## Understanding Muscle Visualization

### The 3D Muscle Model

MuscleMap tracks **40+ individual muscles** across your body:

**Upper Body:**
- Chest (pectorals major/minor)
- Back (lats, traps, rhomboids, erector spinae)
- Shoulders (anterior/lateral/posterior deltoids)
- Arms (biceps, triceps, forearms)

**Core:**
- Abdominals (rectus, obliques, transverse)
- Lower back (erector spinae)
- Hip flexors

**Lower Body:**
- Quadriceps (4 muscles)
- Hamstrings (3 muscles)
- Glutes (maximus/medius/minimus)
- Calves (gastrocnemius, soleus)

## Joining the Community

### Hangouts

**Hangouts** are location-based gym communities. Find people who train at your gym!

1. Go to **Locations** tab
2. Search for your gym or add a new one
3. Join the Hangout
4. See local leaderboards and activity

### Virtual Hangouts

Can't find a local gym? Join **Virtual Hangouts** based on:
- Training style
- Goals
- Archetype
- Schedule

### Rivalries

Challenge someone to a friendly competition:

1. Find a user with similar stats
2. Send a rivalry challenge
3. Set the terms (duration, metric)
4. Compete and track progress!

### Crews

Form or join a **Crew** for team training:
- Shared workouts
- Group challenges
- Team leaderboards
- Crew chat

## Setting Up Your Profile

### Essential Settings

Navigate to **Settings** to configure:

**Privacy:**
- Profile visibility (public/friends/private)
- Activity sharing
- Leaderboard participation

**Notifications:**
- Workout reminders
- Achievement alerts
- Rivalry updates
- Community activity

**Wearables:**
- Connect Apple Watch
- Connect Fitbit
- Connect Garmin
- Enable HealthKit/Google Fit sync

### Your Companion Creature

Your **Companion** evolves as you train:

```
Evolution Stages:
Stage 1: Baby         (0 TU)
Stage 2: Adolescent   (500 TU)
Stage 3: Capable      (2,000 TU)
Stage 4: Armored      (5,000 TU)
Stage 5: Flying       (10,000 TU)
Stage 6: Magnificent  (25,000 TU)
```

Customize your companion with:
- Auras
- Armor
- Wings
- Tools
- Badges
- Abilities
