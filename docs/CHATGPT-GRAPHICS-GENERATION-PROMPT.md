## Brand Identity & Design System

Before generating any graphics, understand the MuscleMap brand:

### Color Palette
- **Primary Blue:** #0066FF (Electric blue - represents energy, focus, depth)
- **Magenta/Pulse:** #FF3366 (Secondary accent - activation, life, energy)
- **Void Black:** #0A0A0F (Deep space background)
- **Text White:** #FFFFFF with 70-50% opacity variants

### Muscle Activation Colors
- Chest: #EF4444 (Red)
- Back: #3B82F6 (Blue)
- Shoulders: #F97316 (Orange)
- Arms: #8B5CF6 (Purple)
- Legs: #22C55E (Green)
- Core: #EAB308 (Yellow)
- Cardio: #EC4899 (Pink)

### Visual Style
- **Aesthetic:** Glassmorphism with backdrop blur
- **Effects:** Bioluminescent glows, liquid glass surfaces
- **Depth:** Multi-layer blur creates z-space perception
- **Mood:** Premium, futuristic, energetic, inspiring
- **Inspiration:** Apple visionOS, iOS 18, spatial computing interfaces

### Typography
- **Headings:** Bebas Neue (bold, athletic)
- **Body:** Inter (clean, readable)
- **Code:** JetBrains Mono
