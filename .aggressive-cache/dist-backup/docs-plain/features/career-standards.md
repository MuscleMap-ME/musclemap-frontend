```

============================

Method: Grease the Groove (GTG)
Duration: 4 weeks

How it works:
├── Test your max (e.g., 32)
├── Do 50% (16) throughout the day
├── 5-8 sets spread across waking hours
├── Never go to failure
└── Retest weekly

Week 1: 50% max, 5 sets/day
Week 2: 55% max, 6 sets/day
Week 3: 60% max, 7 sets/day
Week 4: Deload, retest

Expected improvement: +30-50%
```

## FAQ

### Can I track multiple standards?

Yes. You can set primary and secondary standards. For example, track both ACFT and FBI PFT.

### How often should I do mock tests?

Recommendation: Full mock test monthly, individual events weekly.

### Does this replace official testing?

No. MuscleMap provides training and estimates. Official tests must be conducted through proper channels.

### Can my unit use this for official tracking?

Contact us about enterprise/B2B solutions for official unit fitness tracking.
