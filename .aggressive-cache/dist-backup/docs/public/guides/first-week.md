# Your First Week on MuscleMap

A day-by-day guide to getting started with MuscleMap and building your fitness tracking habits.

---

## Day 1: Setup

```
CHECKLIST:
☐ Create account
☐ Complete onboarding quiz
☐ Choose your archetype
☐ Set your first goal
☐ Explore the dashboard
```

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Visit musclemap.me/signup**
2. **Enter your details:**
   - Email address
   - Strong password
   - Display name (public username)
3. **Verify your email** (check spam if needed)
4. **Complete the onboarding:**
   ```
   Question 1: What's your main goal?
   → Select one: Build muscle / Lose weight / Get fit / etc.

   Question 2: Experience level?
   → Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced

   Question 3: Available equipment?
   → Full gym / Home gym / Bodyweight only

   Question 4: Any limitations?
   → Add injuries or restrictions (optional)
   ```
5. **Choose your archetype** (see [Features Guide](./features.md))
6. **Set a simple first goal:**
   - "Complete 3 workouts this week"
   - "Log 100 TU"

---

## Day 2: First Workout

```
TUTORIAL: Logging Your First Workout
────────────────────────────────────
1. Tap "Start Workout" on dashboard
2. Select "Quick Start" (log as you go)
3. Search for your first exercise
4. Enter sets, reps, weight
5. Watch the muscle map light up!
6. Add more exercises
7. Tap "Finish Workout"
8. Review your summary
9. Celebrate!
```

---

## Day 3: Explore Features

- **Browse the exercise database** - Find new exercises
- **Check your stats** - See your first entries
- **Review muscle coverage** - Any gaps to fill?
- **Look at your companion** - It's just hatched!

---

## Day 4: Connect

- **Find your gym** - Search in Locations
- **Join a Hangout** - Your local community
- **Browse Virtual Hangouts** - Interest groups
- **Give a high-five** - Encourage someone!

---

## Day 5: Build Habits

- **Set workout reminders** - Settings > Notifications
- **Plan your week** - When will you train?
- **Try a prescription** - AI-generated workout

---

## Day 6: Track Progress

- **Review your week** - Stats > Weekly Summary
- **Check muscle balance** - Are you hitting everything?
- **Update your goal** if needed

---

## Day 7: Rest & Reflect

- **Take a rest day** - Recovery is training!
- **Review what you learned**
- **Plan next week**
- **Engage with community** - Comment, high-five
