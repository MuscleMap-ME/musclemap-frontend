## Creating Custom Templates

Save your favorite routines for quick access:

1. **After a workout:** Tap "Save as Template"
2. **Name your template:** "Monday Push Day"
3. **Add notes:** "Start with incline for upper chest focus"
4. **Set as default:** Make it your go-to for that day

### Using Templates

- Dashboard > Start Workout > My Templates
- Tap to load
- Modify weights/reps as needed
- Log as usual
