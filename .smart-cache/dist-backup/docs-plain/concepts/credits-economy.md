## Overview

Credits are MuscleMap's virtual currency. They provide a flexible way to access premium features while keeping the core app free.

```
   Achievements    →  Premium features   →  Subscribe

```

## FAQ

### Do credits expire?

No. Purchased credits never expire. Use them whenever you want.

### Can I get a refund?

- **Subscriptions**: Cancel anytime, no refund for current period
- **Credit purchases**: Non-refundable
- **Contact support** for billing issues

### Why can't I afford a workout?

You need 25 credits. Options:
- Earn through achievements
- Purchase a credit pack
- Subscribe for unlimited
- Use free alternatives (repeat workouts, manual logging)

### Are credits transferable between accounts?

No. Credits are tied to your account. You can gift to others but not transfer to another account you own.

### What happens to my credits if I delete my account?

Deleting your account permanently removes all data including credits. They cannot be recovered or refunded.

### Is there a way to earn unlimited free credits?

No unlimited free credits, but:
- Consistent play earns achievement credits
- Referrals earn credits
- You can use free features indefinitely
