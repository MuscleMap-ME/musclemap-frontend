## High Fives

### What Are High Fives?

High fives are quick encouragement gestures:

```
HIGH FIVE TYPES
===============
+--------+------------------------+
| Type   | Meaning               |
+--------+------------------------+
| Single | General encouragement |
| Double | Extra enthusiasm      |
```

### Sending High Fives

- Tap the high five button on any activity
- Quick tap = single high five
- Long press = choose special type
- Send after workouts, PRs, achievements

- Level 4+
- 50 credits
- Unique name

Steps:
1. Go to **Community → Crews**

## Rivalries

### How Rivalries Work

```
RIVALRY FLOW
============

1. CHALLENGE    You challenge another user
      |
2. ACCEPT       They accept the terms
      |
3. COMPETE      Both train during period
      |
4. CONCLUDE     Winner determined by metric
      |
5. REWARD       Bragging rights (and credits if wagered)
```

### Setting Up a Rivalry

1. Visit rival's profile
2. Tap **Challenge**
3. Configure:
   - Duration: 1 week / 2 weeks / 1 month
   - Metric: TU / Workouts / Specific lift
   - Stakes: None / Credits (25-500)
4. Send challenge

## FAQ

### How do I make my profile private?

Settings → Privacy → Enable Minimalist Mode

### Can I leave a crew?

Yes. Go to Crew → Settings → Leave Crew. If you're captain, transfer ownership first.

### How do rivalries handle ties?

The challenger wins ties. If you challenged, you win. If challenged, you lose.

### Why can't I find someone?

They may have:
- Private profile
- Minimalist mode enabled
- Blocked you
- Different username than expected
