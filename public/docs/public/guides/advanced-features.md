## Wearable Integration

### Connecting Apple Watch

1. Go to **Settings > Wearables**
2. Tap **Connect Apple Watch**
3. Approve HealthKit permissions
4. Sync automatically begins

**What syncs:**
- Heart rate during workouts
- Active calories
- Workout detection
- Steps and movement

### Connecting Fitbit

1. Go to **Settings > Wearables**
2. Tap **Connect Fitbit**
3. Log into Fitbit account
4. Approve permissions
5. Data syncs every 15 minutes
