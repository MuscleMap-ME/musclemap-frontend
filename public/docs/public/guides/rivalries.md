# Rivalries & Competitions

Compete with others to stay motivated and push your limits.

---

## Starting a Rivalry

### Step 1: Find an Opponent

**Option A: From Leaderboards**
- Go to Leaderboards
- Find someone near your rank
- Tap their profile > Challenge

**Option B: From Hangout**
- Go to your Hangout
- Browse members
- Find someone with similar stats
- Tap Challenge

**Option C: Search**
- Go to Search
- Enter username
- Tap Challenge

### Step 2: Set Terms

```
┌────────────────────────────────────┐
│      CHALLENGE @FitnessFreak       │
├────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Competition Metric:                │
│ ○ Total TU                         │
│ ● Workout Count                    │
│ ○ Specific Exercise                │
├────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Duration:                          │
│ ○ 3 Days                           │
│ ● 1 Week                           │
│ ○ 2 Weeks                          │
│ ○ 1 Month                          │
├────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Stakes: [100] credits              │
│ (You'll both put up 100)           │
├────────────────────────────────────┤
│        [SEND CHALLENGE]            │
└────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Step 3: Wait for Response

- They have 48 hours to respond
- You'll get notified either way
- Challenge expires if no response

### Step 4: Compete!

- Rivalry starts when accepted
- Track progress in Rivalries tab
- Get notified of opponent's activity
- Winner takes all credits!

---

## Rivalry Tips

| Strategy | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Choose wisely** | Pick someone at your level for fair competition |
| **Stay consistent** | Daily workouts beat sporadic big sessions |
| **Watch their progress** | Use notifications to stay motivated |
| **Be a good sport** | High-five your opponent regardless of outcome |

## Community Competitions

Join organized events and challenges:

### Types of Competitions

- **Time-limited events** - Complete specific goals within a timeframe
- **Theme challenges** - Focus on specific muscles or exercise types
- **Community-wide** - Everyone competes together
- **Archetype battles** - Compete against other archetypes

### How to Join

1. Go to Competitions tab
2. Browse active events
3. Tap "Join Competition"
4. Complete requirements before deadline
5. Check leaderboard for standings
